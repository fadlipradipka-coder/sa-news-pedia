Crimson Order Da: Inside San Andreas' Elusive Syndicate

Crimson Order Da is an international organized crime syndicate. Originating in Birmingham, England, in 1998, the organization has evolved into a global enterprise with deep roots in San Andreas.

To the general public in San Andreas, the Order appears as a successful business conglomerate. Their impressive portfolio reportedly includes high-end restaurants, bustling bars, popular local markets, luxury hotels, sprawling casinos, and high-end vehicle dealerships across the state.

However, law enforcement sources indicate these legal fronts serve as a sophisticated, untraceable cover for a vast network of criminal enterprises, moving capital and goods seamlessly between their international territories and San Andreas operations.
